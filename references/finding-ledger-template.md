# Finding Ledger Template

Use this ledger during analysis and revision so every major conclusion stays auditable.

## Required fields

```markdown
### F01 [short finding title]
- Claim type: Fact | Finding | Insight | Hypothesis | Recommendation
- Claim: [bounded statement]
- Audience relevance: Leadership | Product | Design | Operations
- Evidence:
  - Quantitative: [metric, denominator, subgroup, source]
  - Qualitative: [quote id, scene, respondent profile]
- Confidence: High | Medium | Low
- Boundary: [for whom / under what condition]
- Counter-signal: [contradictory pattern, exception, or caveat]
- Alternative explanation: [other plausible read]
- Recommendation link: [if any]
- Status: Draft | Reviewed | Revised | Final
```

## Usage rules

- Every executive finding should map back to a ledger entry.
- If a claim cannot be entered cleanly, it is probably too vague.
- Keep the counter-signal field even when the main pattern is strong.
